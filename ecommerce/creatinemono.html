More Information:

Endogenously, Creatine is biosynthesised in the liver from three amino acids - Arginine, Glycine and Methionine. This creatine, or the type taken as a supplement, is then phosphorylated to form Creatine Phosphate, (CP) the essential ingredient in the ATP shuttle that converts ADP to ATP, and then back to ADP with the release of the immediate energy needed for quick burst activity such as lifting a weight or sprinting.
